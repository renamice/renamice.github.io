Hey There!

My name is Rijan Tamang (Rena).

I am a student with a keen interest in computer science. I have been self-teaching myself things like programming from a young age, and I love working on projects that challenge me to learn and grow.

Most of my projects are small bits of code, which have helped me learn and grow. I upload projects that I feel confident sharing on my GitHub; as of now, there aren’t a lot of them.

The first language I ever learned was Python, which I learned while in middle school. I didn’t go much deeper into the topic since it was too much for me to cover at that time, especially the object-oriented portions of the language. I am particularly interested in how computers work under the hood. The low-level working of computer systems, specifically, intrigues me very much.

I think that understanding the fundamentals helps in better understanding the topics than just relying on abstractions. The approach drives me to explore various topics that I find myself interested in.

I am also interested in reading books and taking photos. I share my thoughts on a few of the books that I read on my blogs, and I upload a few of my photos on my Instagram.

Feel free to reach out to me via email or on my social media.

As time goes on, this website will grow alongside me. So, maybe check in after a few years or so. Perhaps I will have more interesting things to show you then…
